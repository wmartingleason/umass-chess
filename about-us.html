About Us

According to current president Alexander Wang, the chess club is "old as hell," so we don't know for sure when it was founded. He said he thinks it was founded prior to the 1970's. Our mission is to spread the love of chess throughout UMass and the Amherst community, and we do this by putting on tournaments for the community, and by getting our president to dress up in a silly pawn costume.
